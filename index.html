gAME DESCRIPTION

Okara Escape is a heart-pounding escape game that plunges you into a world of puzzles, secrets, and ancient mysteries. Trapped within the mythical city of Okara, you must solve intricate puzzles, decipher cryptic messages, and unlock hidden paths to escape. Each level takes you deeper into the city's enigmatic past, testing your wits and courage at every turn. Prepare to immerse yourself in a game where every choice matters, every puzzle reveals a story, and every step leads you closer to freedom—or deeper into Okara’s grip.
